About Products

FixNix Enterprise GRC - The Product FixNix GRC is the Information Security Management System product specially designed to cater the needs of Small and Medium Enterprises. With FixNix GRC you can easily manage policies, risks, audits, vulnerabilities, identities and workflow management. FixNix GRC solves the most complex and sensitive security challenges.

FixNix Risk MON - provides an integrated, holistic view of risks facing the enterprise that serves as an aggregation and visualization point for an organization risk management program, allowing you to bring risk information together from disparate, siloed risk repositories and identify, assess, decision, treat and monitor risks from one solution. This integrated approach enables you to analyze multiple risks across organizational silos and provide actionable insights to help optimize the performance within a dynamically changing business climate.

FIXNIX IT GRC - keeps away the organization exposure from risk, threats, vulnerabilities, that have a direct impact on the performance of the enterprise. IT GRC helps in planning, designing and administrate the IT systems.
